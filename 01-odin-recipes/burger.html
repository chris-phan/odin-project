Burger Recipe

Description

This is a burger recipe but not just any ordinary burger recipe. This is a burger passed down from generation to generation. It has its roots in the Jurassic Period and with the stegosaurus people that my ancestors befriended. It is with great honor that I am sharing it with you today, and I am beyond thrilled that this great tradition will continue on to the future where it can be enjoyed by all chickens, no matter the color of their feathers or the size of their beaks. Please enjoy.

Steps

  1. place bottom bun on surface
  2. place patty on top of bottom bun
  3. place lettuce on top of patty
  4. place tomatoes, pickles, and onions on top of lettuce
  5. put ketchup and mustard on top bun
  6. take a bite out of the top bun and place it on top other half of the burger
  7. eat burger and repeat 20 more times

Ingredients

  • 2 burger buns
  • 1 patty
  • 5 pieces of lettuce
  • 2 slices of tomato
  • 2 pieces of pickle
  • diced and grilled onions
  • ketchup
  • mustard
  • cheese (if you're into that sort of stuff)
  • a hunger fit for dinosaurs and a belly to match
