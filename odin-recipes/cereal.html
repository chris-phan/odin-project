Cereal Recipe

Description

There will be no jokes for this recipe since this is a very serious topic. The age old debate on whether cereal should be pour first or if milk should be poured first has gone on for far too long and needs to be settled. I will be representing the side of the milk firsters and by the end of this, you will have to be missing at least 72% of your brain mass to still be on the side of the cereal firsters. My argument is composed of two parts. Firstly, pouring milk first means that when you are ready to pour the cereal into the bowl, you will pour a small amount each time to ensure that the cereal stays crispy and doesn't end up as a soggy mess. In this method, the milk to cereal ratio is high as opposed to pouring milk into your cereal where it is incredibly easy to pour too much milk, making your cereal have the texture of pre-chewed food or your cereal has too little milk, making some parts of your cereal dry and hard and others soggy and frankly disgusting. Those that never pour cereal into milk either never go for a second serving of cereal or drink all the milk before pouring cereal into an empty bowl. If you are the latter, I'm afraid that you have fallen victim to my trap as the only possible down side of pouring milk first is that you may end up with left over milk, but if you enjoy drinking the milk at the end, there is no downside for being a milk firster. All in all, pouring milk first leaves you with more options as you can easily go for seconds and you also have more room for error since it is much easier to pour a little bit of cereal at a time versus pouring the exact right amount of milk the first time if you are a disgusting, vile, ignorant, and criminal cereal firster. Secondly, people that pour cereal first have been found to be highly coorelated to premature balding and stinky breath.

Ingredients

  • cereal
  • milk

Steps

  1. pour milk in bowl
  2. pour cereal in bowl
  3. eat all cereal and pour more if still hungry
