Water Recipe

Description

Water is one of the most obscure drinks in the entire world. According to a survey conducted in 2017 with over 500,000,000 participants, all of which were American honey bees, it was found that 100% of respondants said that water was the last thing that they would ever drink. This survey has been the subject of much controversy and has caused the global population of humans to band together and reject water, choosing to drink Pepsi instead. Due to this unprecedented shift in public opinion, I thought that it would only be right to document these events and give an honest and informed recipe on how water is made illegally in the black markets of Davis, California.

Steps

  1. collect water using an instrument of your choice*
  2. pour into cup
  3. preheat oven to 400 C
  4. line oven tray with aluminum foil and pour water from cup onto tray
  5. place tray in oven for 12 minutes
  6. remove tray from oven and enjoy
